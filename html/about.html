About Me

This is what I do.

My name is Joseph Harris, but all of my friends call me JoJo. I am the second oldest of 6 kids, and my family means everything to me. I love my parents, my older brother Nathaniel is my role model, James is my roomate and best friends, and Lizzie, Andrew, and Joshua are all amazing and just the absolute best! I was born in Silver Spring Maryland, just outside of Washington DC. Since then I have live in Wichita Kansas for 10 years, Idaho Falls throughout high school, New York City for 2 years while I served a mission for my church, and now live in Provo Utah. You can see all of these amazing places that I have had the chance to call home in the backgrounds of the pages on this website.

I just finished my sophmore year at Brigham Young University, my dream school! I love BYU, and am a huge BYU sports fan. I try to attend as many games as possible. I also love living in Provo and all of my friends who live here. My current major is pre-business, and I will be applying to the Marketing, Global Supply Chain, and Experience and Design Management majors at the end of this month!

I love to play any and all sports, but expecially soccer! I have played soccer since I was 4 years old and LOVE it. I also love anything outside. I love boating, skiing, hiking, and going on trips with all of my amazing friends I've made here in Provo. I'm a huge fan of watching movies and tv shows and am a huge Star Wars, Harry Potter, and Lord of the Rings nerd!
